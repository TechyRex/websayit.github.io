Become a Coach.

Volunteer to Become a Coach At Soulaze, we believe in the power of shared experiences and the importance of mental well-being. Our platform is designed to empower individuals to express their thoughts, feelings, and experiences in a safe, supportive environment, where their voices are not only heard but understood.

As a coach on Soulaze, you will play a pivotal role in fostering this environment.
We are looking for passionate, empathetic individuals who are dedicated to making na positive impact on others' lives by providing guidance, support, and a listening ear.

Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Soulaze, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy while using our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email, and phone number when you volunteer as a counselor. This information is used to maintain communication and offer you relevant opportunities.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information will only be used for administrative purposes and to connect you with those seeking counseling services. We will not share your personal details with any third parties without your explicit consent.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to ensure the security of your data, both online and offline. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Rights to Access and Control Your Data

You have the right to access, update, and delete any personal information we have on file. You may contact us at any time to request these changes.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated via email and updated on our website.

By using Soulaze and volunteering as a counselor, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

What Happens Next?

Once your application has been submitted, our team will carefully review and vet all the information provided. Upon approval, you will receive an email with further details and next steps.

We appreciate your commitment to mental well-being and your desire to help others through counseling.
