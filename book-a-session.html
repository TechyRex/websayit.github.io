Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. At Soulaze, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy while using our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you book a session on Soulaze, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name (Optional)
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Details of your feelings and the message to the counselor

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to connect you with the appropriate counselor and to ensure you receive the support you need. We do not share your information with any third parties unless required by law.

3. Data Security

Your information is stored securely and protected with appropriate technical measures to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Confidentiality

All sessions are confidential. The details you share with Soulaze and your counselor are kept private unless you explicitly agree otherwise, or if there is a legal obligation to disclose information.

5. Rights to Access and Control Your Data

You have the right to access, update, and delete any personal information we have on file. You may contact us at any time to request these changes.

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be communicated via email and updated on our website.

By booking a session on Soulaze, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
